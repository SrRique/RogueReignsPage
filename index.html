World Inhabitants

Asteria

An enigmatic sage who remains untouched by the corruption that plagues the world. She is the first soul the heroes encounter upon awakening from their tormented slumber, guiding them toward the old rulers who guard the corrupted pages of the Book of Creation. A beacon of truth in dark times, any secret she harbors must serve the cause of restoration.

Don Diego

Once a distinguished Tarrascosan commander, Don Diego descended into a bloodthirsty beast on the chaotic battlefields of the fallen era. When his conscience suddenly returned during a particularly gruesome battle, he was horrified by the monster he had become. Now disturbed by his gory past, he seeks redemption by teaching the promised heroes the art of warfare.

Fildea

Former court huntress to the Archducal house of Everyseason, she fled to the wilderness hoping to escape the age of madness. But corruption's reach proved inescapable. Instead of mere survival, she transformed into an apex predator, slaughtering every beast that dared cross her path with ruthless precision. When a mysterious force compelled her to return to the ruins of the civilization she had abandoned, she found new purpose helping the heroes battle the creatures that threaten creation itself.
